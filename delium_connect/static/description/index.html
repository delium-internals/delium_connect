FAQ's
1. What does this app do ?
This app acts as a conduit to register yourselves to our product The Miner. Your data is pulled on a regular basis and used to generate useful information that we show on a dedicated website created and shared to you. Rest assured, your data is safe with us. All data pulled is encrypted before storing. There is no external/ third party accesses allowed to your data. Your data is solely used only for the generation of information on the Miner website. For more information regarding terms of usage, refer to https://www.delium.ai/pages/terms.pdf
2. What kind of data do we pull ?
Master and transactional data that is required to generate the inferences on the Miner website. For more details on the models and fields, check https://www.dcove.delium.ai.
3. Where do I see my data with the Miner ?
On registration and sync enablement, a separate website will be created. The credentials will be communicated to you as per the registered details. This website will be hosted and managed by us. The data synced will be used to generate inferences that you will be able to see on this website.
4. What is the model of engagement ?
An initial trial of 15 days is provided to try out the system. After the trial ends and an commercial engagement is in place, you will have meetings with our implementation team for a period of 8 weeks. Additional support post this can be discussed on a commercial basis if required.
5. What is the cost for The Miner ?
There is no fixed price for The Miner as data size is a major factor for cost. As such, the actual commercials will be intimated post the trial period. Commercials are subscription based with options to pay monthly or annually.
6. How many users can use The Miner ?
You can create any number of users from the Miner website. Be warned that any user added will have access to the information shown on the Miner website. User management on the Miner website is fully owned by you.
